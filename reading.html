Reading

  2. Speed of Dark by Elizabeth Moon
  3. Call it What You Want by Brigid Kemmerer
  4. With the Fire on High by Elizabeth Acevedo
  5. The Seven Rules of Elvira Carr by Frances Maynard
  6. Scythe by Neal Shusterman
  7. I'll Be There by Holly Goldberg Sloan
  8. Allegedly by Tiffany Jackson
  9. Who Put This Song On by Morgan Parker
  10. The Truth Is by NoNieqa Ramos
